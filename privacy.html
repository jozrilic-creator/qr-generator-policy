Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how QR Generator by Josipa Design handles user data and protects your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect or share personal information. All QR generation happens locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

The app may store QR history and logo selections locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

3. Advertising

The free version uses Google AdMob for banner ads. AdMob may collect non-personal identifiers such as advertising IDs for ad relevance.

Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

  • Storage access – to save generated QR code images
  • Read documents – to select logo images

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 years old and does not collect information from them.
